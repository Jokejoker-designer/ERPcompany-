THANH HOAI ERP - BO HO SO CONG TRINH V3.1 WINDOWS SAFE

Ly do phat hanh V3.1:
- V3 su dung ten thu muc/file co dau tieng Viet va duong dan dai.
- Mot so cong cu giai nen cua Windows hien thi sai ma ky tu hoac khong mo duoc duong dan dai.
- V3.1 chi dung ky tu ASCII trong TEN THU MUC VA TEN FILE.
- Noi dung ben trong DOCX/XLSX van giu tieng Viet Unicode.

Cach su dung:
1. Tao mot thu muc ngan, vi du: D:\TH_ERP
2. Chep file ZIP vao thu muc do.
3. Bam chuot phai > Extract All.
4. Khong giai nen vao duong dan qua dai nhu Desktop\Tai lieu\...\nhieu cap.
5. Mo file README va PATH_MAP_VI_TO_ASCII.csv de xem ten tieng Viet tuong ung.

Cam ket tuong thich:
- Tat ca ten duong dan trong ZIP chi co ASCII.
- Khong co ky tu cam cua Windows.
- Duong dan noi bo duoc gioi han.
- Registry ERP da cap nhat sang duong dan ASCII.
